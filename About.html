About SecureID Pro

The most advanced privacy protection tool for sensitive documents, using cutting-edge AI and computer vision technologies.

Our Mission: Protect Privacy in the Digital Age

In today’s hyper-connected world, your personal information is more valuable than gold — and just as vulnerable. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to safeguard your identity by ensuring sensitive details stay hidden from prying eyes.

With cutting-edge OCR technology, smart AI detection, and precision masking, we transform scanned ID documents into privacy-shielded versions — without compromising usability. Whether it’s Social Security Numbers, names, or other personal details, we ensure only what’s necessary is shared, and the rest stays locked away. Because in the digital age, privacy isn’t a luxury — it’s your right.

Our Journey

2018 - Foundation

SecureID Pro was founded by a team of cybersecurity experts and AI researchers who saw the growing need for accessible privacy tools.

2019 - First Prototype

Developed the initial document masking algorithm that would become the core of our technology.

2020 - Beta Launch

Released our first beta version to select security professionals and privacy advocates for testing.

2021 - Version 1.0

Officially launched SecureID Pro to the public with advanced OCR and pattern detection capabilities.

2023 - AI Revolution

Integrated deep learning models for significantly improved detection accuracy and new masking methods.

Our Technology

Advanced OCR

Combines multiple OCR engines with custom-trained models for maximum text recognition accuracy.

AI Detection

Deep learning models trained on thousands of documents to identify sensitive patterns and entities.

Local Processing

All analysis happens in your browser - no data ever leaves your device for maximum security.

Multiple Masking

Choose from various masking techniques including blur, pixelation, pattern redaction, and blackout.

Continuous Learning

Our models improve with each document processed, adapting to new document formats and patterns.

Military-Grade

Uses encryption and security protocols developed for government and financial institutions.

Real-Time Detection Preview

Instantly highlights sensitive areas in uploaded images, allowing users to confirm or edit detected regions before masking.

Manual Review & Adjustments

Empowers users to manually add or remove mask areas for precise control over AI-detected regions.

Multi-Language OCR Support

Detects and masks names and SSNs across various languages and fonts for global applicability.

Custom Masking Rules

Allows users to define custom keywords or regex patterns for masking specific data like Driver License or Passport Numbers.

Batch Processing Mode

Enables users to upload and process multiple ID images simultaneously for efficient workflows.

Download in Multiple Formats

Save masked outputs as PDF, PNG, or JPEG, with optional before/after comparison views.

Audit Report Generation

Generates detailed privacy audit reports listing detected and masked items for transparency.

Offline Desktop/Web App Mode

Supports fully offline operation using Electron for desktop or Progressive Web App features.

Masking Confidence Indicator

Displays confidence scores for each detection, ensuring users understand AI certainty levels.

Accessibility & Compliance

Designed for WCAG accessibility standards and compliant with GDPR and HIPAA regulations.
